The Beginning

How It All Started

It started with a simple, burning question: why couldn't Kathmandu have a burger as good as any in New York or London? Our founder, a passionate home cook turned entrepreneur, spent two years perfecting recipes, sourcing the best local ingredients, and training a team that would become a family. In 2018, The Burger House & Crunchy Hub opened its doors — and the city hasn't stopped talking since.

What began as a 20-seat spot in Thamel has grown into one of Kathmandu's most celebrated dining destinations, with a reputation that draws visitors from across Nepal and beyond. But through all of it, the mission remains the same: make the best burger you've ever had, every single time.

The Journey

Our Timeline

1
2018
The First Flame
Doors open in Thamel with a 20-seat space, 8 burgers on the menu, and a dream. First week: sold out every day.
2
2019
The Crunchy Hub is Born
Expanded the kitchen, added the full sides and snacks menu — the "Crunchy Hub" concept arrives. Capacity doubles to 45 seats.
3
2020
Resilience & Reinvention
Pandemic pivot: launched our home delivery service and DIY burger kits that became viral. Kept the whole team employed.
4
2022
Interior Redesign & Awards
Complete interior renovation — industrial-chic design by local architect Aayush Rai. Awarded "Best Burger in Kathmandu Valley" by Food & Travel Nepal.
5
2025
10,000 Happy Guests & Growing
Over 10,000 guests served. Menu expanded to 50+ items. Still using the same original smash burger recipe from day one.
